GLACIER PEAK
Glacier Peak
37º 43’ 50’’ N, 119º 34’ 25’’ W
Glacier Point, an overlook with a commanding view of Yosemite Valley. Half Dome, Yosemite’s high country, is accessible by car from approximately late May through October or November. From mid-December through March, cross-country skiers can experience this view after skiing 10.6 miles.
